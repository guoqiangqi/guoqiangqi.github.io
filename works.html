02 : Works

I graduated in 2020 with a master's degree, and then I entered the Huawei 2012 laboratory to work. In the second year of graduate school, I had an internship at the Hong Kong Research Institute of Tcl Industrial Research Institute, mainly responsible for the Animoji project based on deep learning. At Huawei, I am mainly responsible for the optimization of open-source mathematics libraries in the field of scientific computing, such as Eigen and Numba. I am also a member of Eigen.

I'm a open source enthusiast, familiar with Deep Learning(Object Detection)/AutoML(NAS, Darts) and Numerical Optimization. I'm currently working on Eigen(member of Eigen), which is a C++ template library for linear algebra: matrices, vectors, numerical solvers, and related algorithms, also is used in Tensorflow. Also, i'm wroking on Numba, which is a JIT compiler that translates a subset of Python and NumPy code into fast machine code for scientific computing. I own the PFLD which is an open source implementation based on Tensorflow for Face Landmark Detection.
